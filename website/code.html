Our Story

A Family Recipe,
A Community Treat

Cookie&Co started in a tiny kitchen with a single batch of chocolate chip cookies and a dream. Today we bake hundreds of cookies each morning — but the recipe, and the love behind it, has never changed. Every ingredient is sourced fresh, every dough is mixed by hand.

🌾

Stone-ground flour, locally sourced

We partner with a family farm just 30 miles away for premium milled flour.

🧈

Real butter, no shortcuts

Belgian chocolate chips
